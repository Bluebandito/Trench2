Who is Twenty One Pilots?

Twenty one pilots is a two-member band from Columbus, Ohio. They focus on playing music that is different from the 'modern pop songs'. They also like to advocate against suicide, adressing many different deeper topics in their lyrics.

Formed in 2009 by frontman Tyler Joseph and former members Nick Thomas and Chris Salih. Since 2011, the lineup has consisted of singer/guitarist Tyler Joseph and drummer Josh Dun
