• Studio albums

Vessel (2013)

Vessel is the band's third album, and their first studio album. It was released in 2013 and includes a few remastered songs from regional at best such as "Car radio", "Holding on to you" and "Ode to sleep", as well as other original tracks.

Blurry Face (2015)

Blurry Face is the band's fourth album, and their second studio album. It was released in 2015 and includes the band's most popular songs such as "Stressed out", "Ride" and "Tear in my heart".

This album is considered their debut studio album and received critical acclaim and commercial success, helping to establish Twenty One Pilots as a major force in the music industry.

This album starts the 10+ year story and lore behind the songs that has captivated fans around the world.

Trench (2018)

Trench is the band's fifth album, and their third studio album. It was released in 2018 and includes songs such as "Jumpsuit", "Nico and the Niners" and "Levitate".

This album continues the story and lore that started in Blurry Face, and is considered one of the band's best albums by fans and critics alike.

The album was a commercial success, debuting at number two on the US Billboard 200 chart and receiving positive reviews from critics.

The album's themes include mental health, self-discovery, and the struggle to find one's place in the world.

Scaled and Icy (2021)

Scaled and Icy is the band's sixth album, and their fourth studio album. It was released in 2021 and includes songs such as "Shy away", "Choker" and "Saturday".

This album is a departure from the darker themes of their previous albums, and is considered a more upbeat and poppy album.

The album was a commercial success, debuting at number three on the US Billboard 200 chart and receiving positive reviews from critics.

The album's themes include hope, resilience, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Clancy (2021)

Clancy is the band's seventh album, and their fifth studio album. It was released in 2021 and includes songs such as "Next semester", "Navigating" and "Overcompensate".

This album is a continuation of the story and lore that started in Blurry Face and continued in Trench, and is considered a companion album to Scaled and Icy.

The album was a commercial success, also debuting at number three on the US Billboard 200 chart and receiving positive reviews from critics.

The album's themes include mental health, self-discovery, and the struggle to find one's place in the world, similar to the themes of their previous albums.

Breach (2022)

Breach is the band's eighth album, and their sixth studio album. It was released in 2022 and includes songs such as "City Walls", "Center mass" and "The Contact".

This album continues the story and lore that started in Blurry Face, and is considered a companion album to Scaled and Icy and Clancy.

Debuting at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart, the album was a very big success.
